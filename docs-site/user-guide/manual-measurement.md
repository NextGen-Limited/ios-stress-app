# Manual Measurement

You can trigger an immediate data fetch from Apple Health at any time.

## How to Take a Manual Reading

1. Open StressMonitor on your iPhone
2. Tap the **refresh icon** on the Dashboard
3. The app fetches the latest HRV and heart rate samples from Apple Health
4. Your stress score updates within a few seconds

## What Manual Measurement Does

Manual measurement reads the most recent samples stored in Apple Health — it does not instruct Apple Watch to take a new sensor reading. If Apple Watch has not collected new data recently, the reading reflects the most recent available sample.

## When to Use It

- After waking from sleep (fresh overnight HRV available)
- After a period of rest or meditation
- When the automatic background refresh feels stale

## Accuracy Note

For the most accurate stress assessment, measure after at least 5 minutes of rest. Physical activity immediately before checking will temporarily elevate heart rate and may not reflect your baseline state.
