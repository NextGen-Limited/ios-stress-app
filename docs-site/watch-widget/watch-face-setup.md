# Watch Face Setup & Troubleshooting

To add StressMonitor complications to your Apple Watch face, two requirements must be met:

1. **StressMonitor Watch App is installed** on your Apple Watch
2. **watchOS 9 or higher** is installed

## Adding a Complication

We recommend configuring watch faces **directly on your Apple Watch** rather than through the iPhone Watch app to avoid compatibility issues:

1. On your Apple Watch, press and hold the watch face to enter edit mode
2. Tap **Edit**
3. Swipe to the **Complications** tab
4. Tap a complication slot
5. Scroll to find **StressMonitor** and tap to select

## Common Issues

**"Complication unavailable" message**

This is a known watchOS compatibility issue. Configure the complication directly on the watch (steps above) rather than through the iPhone Watch app.

**Watch face elements missing or blank after adding**

Usually temporary after a watch app install or update. Try restarting your Apple Watch. If it persists, remove and re-add the complication directly on the watch.

**StressMonitor doesn't appear in the complication list**

Verify the Watch app is actually installed: open the **Watch** app on iPhone → My Watch → check StressMonitor shows as installed. If not, tap Install. Due to Apple's system delays, watch app installation can take several minutes.

**Watch app fails to install**

- Confirm watchOS version is 9 or higher
- Ensure both iPhone and Watch are connected to Wi-Fi
- Watch must be unlocked and not in special modes
- The download progress bar is unreliable — just wait without repeatedly tapping

## Supported Watch Faces

StressMonitor complications work on any watch face that supports third-party complications, including Modular, Infograph, Infograph Modular, California, Meridian, Solar Dial, and Numerals Duo.
